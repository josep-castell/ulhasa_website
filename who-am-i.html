Ulhasa Kokorō

ULHASA in sanskrit means JOY.

It's my spiritual name, my path. My life's meaning! The joy lights up my course, I walk with the light of joy.

I graduated in architecture, I always loved crafts and handwork. I became a massage therapist and I jumped into the world of Shiatsu.

"I always loved crafts and handwork"

In Shiatsu I started with ZEN line. I deeped into the Shiatsu in movement and now I connect with my roots with Namikoshi Shiatsu.

I would like to share my own vision of Wellness with Healthy Gastronomy (concepts and recipes), Meditation (active techniques) and Activities (alternative events and chats).

I realized that with my hands I can share my energy. I can help people to relax, I can make a bread or I can build a theatre scenary.

Join me in the Path of Joy!
