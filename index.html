SHIATSU

In Japanese means finger pressure. It is a body therapy created in Japan.

It uses hand and finger pressure, stretching and mobilization to correct irregularities, to keep and improve health and to relieve certain diseases — activating the human body's autohealing capability.

It was Tokujiro Namikoshi who elaborated a method for Shiatsu application teaching.
